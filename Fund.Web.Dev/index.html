A Brief History of Airsoft:

In the Beginning – 
Airsoft started as a hobby in Japan, where ownership of firearms is essentially banned. Because of this, very realistic replica firearms that shot plastic in a safe manner were created for firearm enthusiasts.
Over time, these Airsoft Guns have been adapted into a force-on-force sport spanning the globe. In fact, it is now common to see police and military organizations using Airsoft for training situations. As Airsoft became widely accepted and recognized, many were left wondering how to enter this new sport.
Thankfully, it is now easier than ever before, due to the advantages of Technology.

What this means for you:

Those looking to get into Airsoft don't need much more than some funds and an internet connection. Using a small amount of time to browse this site, however, may save you money and effort down the line.
